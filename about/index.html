关于

Feng CHEN （陈 峰）

Ph.D. HKUST

Analog and Power Management IC Designer

BIOGRAPHY

Feng Chen received the B.Eng. degree in electronic and information engineering from Zhejiang University (ZJU), Hangzhou, China, in 2015, and the Ph.D. degree in electronic and computer engineering from The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), Hong Kong, China, in 2020.
He is currently with Silergy Corp., Hangzhou, China. His current research interests involve power management IC (PMIC) design and analog and mixed-signal IC design, especially analog and digital linear regulator.
Dr. Chen was a recipient of the HKUST postgraduate studentship from 2015 to 2020.
